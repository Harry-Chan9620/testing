    bye1
    /b/
    Learn to pronounce
    noun
    noun: bye; plural noun: byes; noun: by
    1. 1.
      the transfer of a competitor directly to the next round of a competition in the absence of an assigned opponent.
      "he has a bye into the second round"
    2. 2.
      Cricket
      a run scored from a ball that passes the batter without being hit (recorded as an extra, not credited to the individual batter).
    3. 3.
      Golf
      one or more holes remaining unplayed after a match has been decided.
    Origin
    mid 16th century (denoting a side issue or incidental matter): from by.

    Is it correct to say by or bye?
    By is versatile, operating as a preposition or adverb to talk about proximity, means, or agents of action. 'Bye,' in contrast, is an informal farewell or a sports term referring to a skipped match. Despite their identical pronunciation, knowing how to use these words correctly is crucial for clear communication.
